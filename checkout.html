Whistler Blackcomb

Whistler Blackcomb located out west near Vancouver is the top choice. It has mega terrain ranging from mild to wild. It offers amazing a diverse range of terrains and a really efficient lift system also including an abundance of off-slope activities.

Revelstoke Mountain Resort

Revelstoke Mountain Resort has been known by cat and heli skiers for the amazing Selkirk powder falling in abundance. It was opened in season of 2007-2008 and continues to develop to turn into a huge resort. This has the potential to be the biggest and best resort in Canada.

Lake Louise

Lake Louise has multi-mountain steep terrain, narrow chutes, fresh powder as well as unbeatable views. This resort went through recent lift upgrades and was voted as “Canada’s Best Ski Resort 2023” according to World Ski Awards. If you stay in Lake Louise Fairmont Chateau Lake Louse is the impeccable sport. If you stay in Banff, the Mount Royal Hotel is unbeatable with chic rooftop hot tubs, mouth-watering cocktails as well as tapas at Brazen and important location to amenities, including buses to all 3 Ski Big 3 resorts.

Marmot Basin

Marmot Basin is located in Banff’s neighbour, Jasper National Park. It is a resort in a national park surrounded with Rocky Mountains and glaciers. The high altitudes provides a lot of ski runs above the tree line and have wide-open spaces for both pros and beginners. The best places to stay here is the Fairmont Jasper Park Lodge.

Kicking Horse Ski Resort

Kicking Horse Ski Resort is located one hour west of Lake Louise, also know for its steep terrain, giant chutes, jagged Rocky Mountain peaks as well as dry fluffy snow. The elevation of the ski resort is an advantage as it receives an abundance of snow. This boast Canada’s highest restaurant and is an epic destination for skiers who are experts and riders. The official accomodation on the mountain is the Glacier Mountain Lodge just few steps from the main gondola.
