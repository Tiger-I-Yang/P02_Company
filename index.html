What does the company do, or offer?

This company coordinatinates and manages consecutive mass donation events that monetarily conect willing individuals' wallets to the most pressing ongoing crises worldwide, such as disaster relief and refugee supply, from a single web interface. Upon entering the site, users are presented with images and descriptions of all the current ongoing crises and conflicts, such as the israel-palestine war, including how the money they donate will aid in the philanthropy

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

This company is a unified accessible outlet for anyone around the world to monetarily support ongoing crises without needing to peel their fat asses from their seats. Just choose an ongoing crisis to support, learn about how your money will be used to help those in need, specify the amount to donate either once or in installments, enter your credit card or paypal information, and confirm.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

It is rooted in culture that donating is a altrusitic and therefore desireable thing to do, but accessible and traustworthy routes of donating to ongoing crises are few and far between. This company plans to make the process as frictionless and straightforward as possible compared to the other avenues of supporting, enabling one to find their bearings and donate within 5 minutes without even having to leave the comfort of their seat.
